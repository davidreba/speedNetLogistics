💡Please consider using Microsoft Copilot instead

ZF now offers a great alternative to ChatGPT & Co: Microsoft Copilot.
It comes with some big advantages: Our personal and company data is protected, and you can even use it with data classified as "confidential".
Microsoft Copilot assists you with various tasks, such as searching for information, summarizing websites/documents/videos, writing and improving texts, generating images, generating code snippets & more. Therefore, we ask you to use Microsoft Copilot instead of public services based on Generative AI.
Visit the quick-start guide of smarter@work to get started with Microsoft Copilot.

If you like to proceed with this website nevertheless, make sure to use public Generative AI tools (like ChatGPT) responsibly and check out the guardrails:
1. External Generative AI tools may only be used with data classified as "public", i.e. data that is publicly available also outside of ZF on the Internet and in the press. All other data marked “internal”, “confidential” and “strictly confidential” – all classified information – must not be used.

2. Results of Generative AI Tools are to be verified and doublechecked by ZF employees to evaluate any threats to our company. There is a risk of infringement of third-party property rights and risks that the output is not accurate nor up to data. The output may not be used directly and unchecked (e.g. for customer projects, AI-generated source code).

3. No legal advice from external Generative AI Tools is allowed. For legal advice please contact the ZF Legal Department.

4. Generative AI Tools must not be used for any business decision making.

5. Content (images, text, ...) generated by Generative AI Tools may not be used for external communication at first.

6. Your interaction must not be used to improve the external Generative AI model. De-activate the settings accordingly (e.g. ChatGPT Settings for data controls > chat history & training). Make sure to not comment and not to rate any results of Generative AI tools (“thumbs up / thumbs down” or use of emoji, etc.). Your feedback is used to train the model.
